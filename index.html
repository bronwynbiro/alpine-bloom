About

Alpine Bloom Bakery creates handcrafted custom cupcakes in Banff using the finest local ingredients. From classic favourites to inventive filled cupcakes, every treat is designed to delight your taste buds.

Located in the heart of Banff, we specialise in custom cupcake orders for birthdays, weddings, and special events. Each creation is unique, just like the occasion it celebrates.

  • Filled cupcakes & unique flavours
  • Made locally in Banff
  • Custom orders & free local delivery

More than just dessert, our cupcakes are a small celebration, a moment of joy, and a piece of edible art you can savour.
